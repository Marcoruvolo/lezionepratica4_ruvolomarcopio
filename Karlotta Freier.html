Karlotta Freier

Karjlotta Freier is an accomplished Brooklyn-based artist with a background in illustration. In 2021, she graduated from the MFA Illustration as Visual Essay program at the School of Visual Arts. In addition to her creative pursuits.

She has taught illustration at the China Academy of Art and spoken as a guest speaker at universities across the United States. Freier's work has also been exhibited in a variety of solo and group exhibitions, as well as festivals worldwide.
